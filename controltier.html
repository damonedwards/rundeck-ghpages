Are you a ControlTier user looking for an alternative or someone who is considering ControlTier?

Depending on your use of ControlTier, Rundeck is a great alternative.

Is Rundeck similar to ControlTier?

Rundeck gives you features similar to ControlTier:

  • Operational console. Like ControlCenter, Rundeck gives you a web portal for self service or centralized administration.
  • Command dispatching. Like CTL, Rundeck lets you execute remote commands and scripts.
  • Jobs and Workflows. Rundeck lets you create sequences of steps into workflows that you can compose like ControlCenter jobs or CTL Workflows.
  • Model-driven automation.Rundeck's distributed management is driven by a resource model of your infrastructure. This model can come from different sources, files, other tools (like Chef or Puppet) or your custom CMDB.

How is Rundeck differnt than ControlTier?

While sharing some roots and philosophy, Rundeck has matured into a tool that complements other tools.

  • Small footprint. Agent-less (via ssh/winrm). Works with third party repositories like yum, Nexus, Artifactory.
  • Simpler. Devleloping automation with Rundeck is generally easier than ControlTier. This is partly due to the fact that Rundeck is Node oriented rather than the complexity of ControlTier's Sites and Services type/object model.
